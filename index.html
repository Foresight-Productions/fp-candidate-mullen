Why I'm Running

Servant leadership, built right here in the Yakima Valley.

"I believe Yakima County deserves leaders who put people first, deliver real results, and stay accountable to the residents they serve." Dave Mullen

Dave Mullen is a lifelong public servant, U.S. Air Force veteran, and proven leader with deep roots in the Yakima Valley. He is running for Yakima County Commissioner, District 1, to bring more than fifteen years of executive experience in municipal administration, healthcare operations, project management, and community development to the county he calls home.

From Eglin Air Force Base to the City of Selah, the Moxee City Council, and the Town of Naches, Dave has stepped into difficult moments and delivered. He stabilizes organizations during leadership transitions, negotiates contracts that save taxpayers millions, builds the trust of city, county, and state partners, and keeps services moving for the people who depend on them.

Today, Dave continues to invest in the Yakima Valley as a youth sports coach, a longtime church board member, and a husband and father raising his family in the same valley where he grew up. He is asking voters to send him to the courthouse to do for District 1 what he has done in every role he has held: lead with integrity, focus on results, and put people first.

What I'll Focus On

Practical priorities. Measurable results.

01

Fiscal Responsibility

Yakima County families and small businesses deserve a commissioner who treats every taxpayer dollar with respect. As a healthcare value analyst, Dave delivered $3.1 million in verified savings in a single fiscal year through smart contract negotiation and process improvement. He'll bring that same discipline to the county budget.

Stewardship
02

Effective Local Government

From the Moxee City Council to interim leadership of the Town of Naches, Dave has navigated real-world city operations, infrastructure projects, and the relationships between local, county, and state government. He understands how the system actually works, and where it needs to work better.

Operations
03

Public Safety & Infrastructure

Strong communities need reliable infrastructure and uncompromising public safety. Dave brings six years of military security leadership and direct experience overseeing a $5 million wastewater treatment project to the table. He'll prioritize the basics: roads, water, public safety, and the systems residents count on every day.

Foundations
04

People-First Leadership

Government works best when it stays close to the people it serves. Dave has spent more than a decade investing in the Yakima Valley community as a parks director, a youth sports coach, and a board member. He'll bring the same approachable, accessible style of leadership to the county commission.
