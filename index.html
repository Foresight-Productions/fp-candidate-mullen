I Candidate Brief

A career of public service, applied directly to the work of county government.

Name
David Mullen
Office

Party

Home
Yakima Valley, WA

Dave Mullen is a lifelong public servant, U.S. Air Force veteran, and proven executive leader running for Yakima County Commissioner, District 1.

Dave brings more than fifteen years of executive experience in municipal administration, healthcare operations, project management, and community development to his candidacy. His record demonstrates a consistent ability to stabilize organizations, deliver measurable results, reduce costs, and improve services for the residents he serves.

From Eglin Air Force Base to the City of Selah, the Moxee City Council, and the Town of Naches, Dave has stepped into difficult moments and delivered. He stabilizes operations during leadership transitions, negotiates contracts that save taxpayers millions, builds the trust of city, county, and state partners, and keeps services moving for the people who count on them.

He continues to invest in the Yakima Valley as a youth sports coach, a longtime church board member, and a husband and father raising his family in the same valley where he grew up. He is asking voters to send him to the courthouse to do for District 1 what he has done in every role he has held: lead with integrity, focus on results, and put people first.

From the Candidate The job is simple to describe and hard to do well. Work today on what matters today. Build tomorrow with what we leave behind. Stay accountable to the people who sent you.

III Mission & Priorities

What gets the time, the focus, and the discipline of this office.

01
Issue I

Strong Farmers. Supporting Our Agriculture.

Yakima County agriculture is the backbone of our economy and our identity. Dave will fight for the policies, infrastructure, and water access that keep our growers competitive, our farms profitable, and our valley feeding the country. When agriculture wins, the whole county wins.

Anchored in Yakima Valley · lifetime
02
Issue II

Advocate for Water Rights. Protecting Our Water. Protecting Our Future.

Water is the most valuable asset Yakima County has, and we cannot afford a commissioner who treats it as a side issue. Dave will defend our rights, oppose efforts to divert our supply, and advocate for the long-term storage and management our region needs to keep growing.

Track record $5M WWTP oversight · Naches
03
Issue III

Fiscal Responsibility. Managing Tax Dollars With Integrity.

Every taxpayer dollar gets the same scrutiny Dave gave $3.1 million in healthcare contract savings: ask whether it has to be spent, whether it can be spent better, and whether the result is worth the cost. The county budget belongs to the residents who fund it.

Track record $3.1M saved · FY21
04
Issue IV

Stronger Communities. Safe, Vibrant Communities Across Yakima County.

Strong communities need reliable infrastructure, uncompromising public safety, and government that actually shows up. Dave brings six years of military security leadership and direct experience running parks, councils, and town operations across the Yakima Valley. He treats the foundations like the priorities they are.

Track record 6 yrs USAF · 3 cities served
05
Issue V

Local Values, Local Voice. Listening. Leading. Delivering.

The residents of District 1 are not abstractions. They are the families Dave coaches youth sports for, the neighbors at his church, and the people he has lived alongside in the Yakima Valley his entire adult life. They deserve a commissioner who answers the phone, shows up, and delivers.

Track record Coaching since 2014
