About Dave

A career of public service.
Ready to deliver results.

Dave Mullen is a U.S. Air Force veteran and proven executive leader running for Yakima County Commissioner, District 1. He brings more than fifteen years of executive experience in municipal administration, healthcare operations, project management, and community development to his candidacy.

Dave served from 2009 to 2015 as Non-Commissioned Officer in Charge of Resource Protection at Eglin Air Force Base, training more than 100 Airmen of all ranks with a 100% pass rate on end-of-course exams. His leadership earned two Air Force Achievement Medals, the Senior Airman Below-the-Zone promotion, and recognition as a Distinguished Graduate of Airman Leadership School.

After the Air Force, Dave returned to the Yakima Valley and served as Parks & Recreation Director for the City of Selah, was elected to the Moxee City Council, served as Business and Value Analyst at Yakima Valley Memorial Hospital (where he saved $3.1M in a single fiscal year), and was hand-picked as Interim City Administrator for the Town of Naches during a leadership crisis.

He continues to invest in the Valley as a youth sports coach, longtime church board member, and a husband and father raising his family here in the Yakima Valley.

A conservative Republican committed to fiscal responsibility, water rights, public safety, and government that actually works for the people of District 1.
